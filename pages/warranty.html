NZXT Warranty Policy

  1. Warranty Length

    NZXT computer cases, temperature meters, fans, accessories, and cables carry a 2-year warranty from the date of purchase for parts and labor. NZXT E Series power supplies offer a 10-year warranty. NZXT Kraken X Series liquid coolers carry a 6-year warranty and the Kraken M22 carry a 3-year warranty. The N7 Z370 motherboard carries an extended 4-year warranty. All NZXT Certified Refurbished products carry a 1-year warranty from the date of purchase for parts and labor. Any replacement product will be warranted for the remainder of the warranty period or thirty days, whichever is longer. Proof of purchase is required for warranty service.
